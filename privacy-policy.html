Privacy Policy

Gaelic Tracker — Eoin Codes

Last updated: April 2026

The short version: Gaelic Tracker does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device. We do not track you.

1. Who We Are

Gaelic Tracker is developed and maintained by Eoin Codes. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the Gaelic Tracker mobile application available on iOS and Android.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Gaelic Tracker operates entirely on-device. All match data, team information, player names, statistics, and settings you enter are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to any server.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Any match or player data you enter into the app

3. Subscription & Payments

Gaelic Tracker offers optional premium subscriptions processed through Apple App Store (iOS) and Google Play (Android). All payment transactions are handled entirely by Apple and Google respectively. Eoin Codes does not receive, store, or process your payment details.

For information on how Apple and Google handle your payment data, please refer to their respective privacy policies.

4. Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status verification. RevenueCat may process a minimal anonymous identifier to confirm your subscription entitlement. No personally identifiable information is shared. You can review RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

5. Data Storage & Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS and Android storage mechanisms. We have no access to this data. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

6. Children's Privacy

Gaelic Tracker does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. As we collect no personal data from any users, the app is safe for use by all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
